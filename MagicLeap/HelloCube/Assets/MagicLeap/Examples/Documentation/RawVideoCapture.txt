# Example Scene: Raw Video Capture

This example demonstrates the functionality of using the camera
for raw video capturing.  The data received from the camera is
processed with a posterization algorithm and then rendered to a
texture for example purposes. Intrinsic properties of the
camera are shown on the side when recording starts.

## Bumper
- First Press: Displays raw video frames.
- Second Press: Stops displaying raw video frames.
- Repeat
